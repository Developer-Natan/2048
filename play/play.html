How to Play 2048

2048 is a sliding tile puzzle game where the goal is to combine numbered tiles to reach the number 2048. Here's how to play:

Objective

Combine tiles of the same number by sliding them on a 4x4 grid. Your aim is to create a tile with the number 2048.

Gameplay

  2. Slide tiles in any direction. All tiles will move as far as possible in that direction.
  3. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one tile with their values combined.
  4. After every move, a new tile (2 or 4) appears in a random empty space.
  5. Keep merging tiles to create higher numbers, aiming for the 2048 tile.

Tips & Strategies

  • Try to keep your highest-value tile in one corner.
  • Focus on building a chain of tiles leading to your highest number.
  • Avoid sliding tiles in ways that separate large tiles.
  • Plan your moves ahead to prevent filling up the grid.

Game Over

The game ends when there are no more valid moves (the grid is full and no tiles can merge).
